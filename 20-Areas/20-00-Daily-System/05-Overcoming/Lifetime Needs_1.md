# Lifetime Needs

- [ ] Filled daily with God's Spirit, to full capacity
- [ ] Think like Christ (Be full of God's Word) 
- [ ] Use the tools of Christian growth daily (Bible study, meditation, prayer, exercising the Spirit, and fasting)
- [ ] Complete daily priorities (put on full armour of light, be ready to help, constantly ask God for help and success, and live sacrificially)
- [ ] To not be too poor and steal, nor have too much and forget God, but have sufficient to have time and resources to serve God and others, and to support His Work
- [ ] Honour God with my substance, in tithes and offerings
- [ ] Basics (food, water, clothes, shelter, and transport)
- [ ] Health and safety (Healthful Living)
- [ ] Meet my obligations and commitments to the kids, mom, and dad
- [ ] Use my God-given skills to enjoy, create, serve, recreate, and be fully self-expressed in music, writing, speaking, reading/learning, travelling, technology, coaching, sales, entrepreneurship, DIY projects, and personal food production
