- Business Wellness (KPIs)
** Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):  This KPI measures the cost of acquiring a new customer. It helps in evaluating the effectiveness of marketing and sales efforts.
** Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):  CLV helps in understanding the total value a customer brings to the business over their entire relationship with the company. It's important for assessing the long-term sustainability of the business.
** Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):  For startups with subscription-based models, MRR is a crucial KPI that measures the predictable and recurring revenue from subscription-based customers.
** Churn Rate:  Churn rate indicates the percentage of customers who stop using the company's products or services over a given period. It's essential for understanding customer retention and the effectiveness of customer relationship management.
** Burn Rate:  Burn rate refers to the rate at which a startup is spending its available capital over a specific period. It's important for managing cash flow and ensuring sustainable growth.
** Runway:  Runway is the length of time a startup can continue operating before it runs out of money, based on its current burn rate. It's crucial for financial planning and fundraising efforts.
** Conversion Rate:  This KPI measures the percentage of potential customers who take a desired action, such as making a purchase or signing up for a service. It's important for evaluating the effectiveness of sales and marketing efforts.
** Active Users or Monthly Active Users (MAU):  For startups with digital products or services, tracking active users provides insights into user engagement and the growth of the customer base.
** Gross Margin: Gross margin measures the profitability of a company's core business activities, indicating how efficiently the company is producing its products or delivering its services.
** Net Promoter Score (NPS): measures customer satisfaction and loyalty by asking customers how likely they are to recommend the company to others. It provides insights into customer sentiment and can indicate potential for organic growth through referrals.
