# Human Needs (INFJ Jumper)

Oi needs certainty and (Ni) organises known insights, ideas, possibilities, patterns

Di  needs significance and (Ti) decides for self based on personal logic, analytical thinking, problem-solving, efficiency, objectivity

Oe needs variety and (Se) uses sensory information gathering, awareness of surroundings, reactions to external stimuli, applying sensory information in the moment, sports skills and body-movement abilities, and focusing on what is rather than what will be
  
De needs validation and (Fe) uses creating and maintaining harmony with those around, considering how decisions will impact others, making connections, advocating for self, discovering how to meet others' needs, organisational abilities, keeping in tune with others' experience, time and environmental settings
