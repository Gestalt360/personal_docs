# Wellness Indicators

(i) Eligibility for salvation/Staying in the faith (repentance, believing the true Gospel, baptism (keeping the old man buried), obedience, loving God exceedingly, carrying my cross daily, willingness to forsake); 

(ii) Beatitudes (spiritually poor; broken and contrite; lowliness; constant yearning to do what is right before God; a compassionate, generous and forgiving attitude; purity in thought, motive, conduct, speech, and appearance; peacemaking; willingness to suffer wrongfully to obey God; being the salt and light of the works); 

(iii) Adherence to the spirit of the Law (both spiritual & physical observance of the Law; avoiding anger and inwardly cursing at brethren without cause; reconciling quickly with offended brethren; avoiding looking lustfully at women and entertaining lustful thoughts, desires and actions; dealing honestly, with integrity, keeping my word always; resisting not the evil, seeking not retribution and always showing love to strangers and enemies; being charitable without seeking attention to myself; communicating accordingly and often with the Father; fasting properly; seeking the kingdom of God above all else and His righteousness; avoiding judging others; handling God's truths carefully; trusting God for physical and spiritual needs; avoiding false prophets; and building my life on Christ's teachings); 

(iv) Fruit of the Spirit (love/outgoing concern for others; joy/cheerfulness; longsuffering/forbearance; gentleness/usefulness or kindness; goodness/commendable traits; faith/belief or trusting God and on Him; meekness/humiliy or gentleness; temperance/self-control or moderation); 

(v) Fruit of faith (virtue/valour or strong backbone (strength of character); knowledge/knowing and understanding God truths, plans and moral wisdom; temperance/self-control; patience/cheerful or hopeful endurance with constancy; godliness/the fear of God or holiness; brotherly kindness/love of the brethren; charity/unconditional love for both stranger and enemy); 

(vi) Physiological conditions (food and nutrition expenses as a percentage of income; 
adequate housing expenditure as a percentage of income; 
healthcare expenses as a percentage of income); 

(vii) Safety indicators (Neighbourhood crime rate; 
Security measures in place; 
Accident and injury rates; 
Emergency preparedness; 
Personal information protections measures); 

(viii) Connections indicators (
Social support network size (number of close relationships); 
Frequency of social interactions with brethren, family and friends; 
Participation in community or social groups); 

(ix) Competence indicators (
Income level; 
Professional development opportunities; 
Recognition and awards received); 

(x) Personal growth indicators (
Progress towards annual & quarterly goals; 
Creative self-expression 

(xi) Financial indicators: 
Income-to-expenses ratio (balance between income and expenses);  
Net worth (total assets minus total liabilities); 
Savings rate (percentage of income saved); 
Debt-to-income ratio (level of debt relative to income)
