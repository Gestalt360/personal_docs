# Heroes Journey

Be a person of my word using my Ti (by seeking to be blameless and being fully congruent within and without, instead of chasing status and fame)

Give from a place of abundance using my Fe (through being empty of want, instead of manipulation, seeking validation, task-doing and chasing ‘likes”)

Manage fears and anxieties using my Ni (through building an effective personal and business success systems, instead of controlling and having a win-lose mindset)

Be satisfied with reality using my Se (through being surrendered, content, and balanced, instead of being never enough, and seeking bigger and better)
