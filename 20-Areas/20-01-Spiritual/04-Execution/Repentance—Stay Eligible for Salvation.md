# Repentance—Stay Eligible for Salvation

- [ ] ⚫ Stage 0: Worldly Sorrow (At ease, short-lived remorse, then repeating it) 
The Challenge: These are areas where my discipline is currently inconsistent and I am prone to repeating old patterns. 
- [ ] Integrity: I am not consistently keeping my word.
- [ ] Disposition: I struggle with a serious or unhappy demeanor and a lack of joy.
- [ ] Morning Discipline: I am getting distracted immediately after waking up and failing to keep my 6:00 AM wake-up and prayer routine.
- [ ] Work Ethics: I am starting work late, not delivering diligently or excellently to my clients, and failing to anticipate or mitigate risks in a timely manner.
- [ ] Financial Management: I am not managing my personal or business finances properly.
- [ ] 🟤  Stage 1: Carefulness (Despatch, eagerness, earnestness, diligence, haste) 
The Challenge: I am applying earnest care and moving with haste to correct these areas, but they require constant vigilance.
- [ ] Physical Routine: I am stuck in a cycle of sleeping late and waking up late, and not completing my daily priorities.
- [ ] Spiritual Routine: I am applying diligence to stop missing my Bible studies and prayers.
- [ ] Financial Stewardship: I am moving with haste to ensure I pay my tithes in full and on time.
- [ ] 🔴  Stage 2: Clearing of Myself (A plea, an "apology," an answer for self, defense) 
The Challenge: I am at the point of contrite and broken-heartedness, and therefore earnestly seeking God's mercy and gift of repentance).
- [ ] Sabbath Thoughts: I am working hard to keep my thoughts focused on the right things throughout the Sabbath.
- [ ] Family Duty: I am applying earnest care to honour my father and mother.
- [ ] 🟠 Stage 3: Fear (Alarm or fright, exceedingly afraid of the consequences of the sin) 
The Challenge: I have a healthy terror of the failure and curses that come from committing these sins or neglecting these responsibilities).
- [ ] Humility: I am actively guarding against pride.
- [ ] Emotional Control: I am working to answer for and clear myself of all indignation.
- [ ] Sabbath Prep: I am doing all it takes to complete all my work and preparations before the Sabbath starts.
- [ ] Business Stewardship: I am driven by a deep sense of responsibility to manage the business properly, completing the core features of the BizBuilder App (billing, CMJS) and its various modules.
- [ ] 🟡 Stage 4: Vehement Desire (A longing for, an earnest desire to be right) 
The Challenge: I have a profound, earnest longing to replace these weaknesses with spiritual strength.
- [ ] Peace & Faith: I have a vehement desire to stop worrying or being consumed by anxiety; I long to put my full and absolute confidence in God.
- [ ] The Way of Give: I desire to stop "living the way of get" and instead become a man who serves, gives, and communicates effectively.
- [ ] 🟢 Stage 5: Zeal (Heat, ardor, fervent mind, jealousy for God’s way) 
The Challenge: I am attacking these areas with heat and ardor, refusing to tolerate their presence in my life.
- [ ] Industry: I am fighting against slackness and slothfulness; I am eager to generate sufficient income and increase my usefulness.
- [ ] Purity: I am fervently eradicating all forms of uncleanness 
- [ ] Contentment: I'm determined and focused on defeating covetousness.
- [ ] Mental Purity: I am attacking critical and judgmental thoughts with a fervent mind.
- [ ] Internalization: I am zealous about actively internalizing God’s Word and straightening my doctrinal knowledge.
- [ ] Engagement: I am no longer avoiding things; I am zealously dealing with reality and developing my talents to support the Work effectively.
- [ ] Temperance: I am maintaining a "green" status of victory over LPM and maintaining self-control over my appetites.
- [ ] Repentance: I am actively and zealously pursuing the practice of repentance daily.
- [ ] 🏆 Stage 6: Revenge (Vindication, retribution, punishment of the sin) 
The Goal: Total Victory—I am exacting retribution against my old self by consistently producing the opposite behavior—where there was once sin, there is now a strong track record of righteousness and a clean conscience. 
- [ ] 
- [ ] Daily Execution Summary (To move items from Stage 0 toward Stage 6):
- [ ] Journal all victories to turn "worldly sorrow" into "carefulness."
- [ ] Execute the BizBuilder Roadmap to move from "fear" to "zeal."
- [ ] Audit my schedule (08:30–18:00) to ensure Stage 0 distractions are "punished" by disciplined action.
